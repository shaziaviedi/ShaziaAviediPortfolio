Overview

You pose a question on the landing screen, typed or spoken, then summon the ritual field with a circular hand gesture traced in camera space. The summoned ring lifts into a three-dimensional constellation of linked stars hovering in layered depth rather than flipping straight to boxed text answers.

From there you rotate the lattice, widen or tighten the view like you are inspecting an instrument dial, drift until momentum feels honest, pause, and latch onto whichever star earns your lingering attention during the embodied navigation phase rather than tapping a predetermined button strip.

Only after selection does the stacked oracle card surface with verdict language, card titles, and supporting copy that echoes the blunt Magic Eight Ball shorthand while still grounding itself in choreography you just survived instead of instantaneous random chatter.

Conceptual Goals

I wanted divination to read as summons plus traversal instead of snapping to an oracle paragraph the second the page wakes. Gesture thresholds slow you enough that the constellation feels guarded rather than already solved.

The star field behaves like a latent answer sheet where every glowing node hides structured categories you might draw from, so pacing can stretch instead of collapsing into a single deterministic blurt the moment curiosity spikes.

Leaning on familiar blunt-oracle energy without copying plastic toy logic meant reframing that tension into navigation, pause, and intentional selection so the body does part of the meaning-making even when the backend still samples from weighted tables.

Aesthetic Goals

The lighting stack chases a ritual circle that actually reads as hot glass before it breaks into wireframe constellations, then keeps deep blue and violet void space threaded with silver glints and restrained gold trim so text panels never feel pasted on a flat wallpaper.

Particle haze, parallax star dust, and soft bloom sell depth without turning the scene into a noisy VFX reel. Geometry also borrows quiet Indonesian rhythm through interlocking arcs and lattice tension in UI chrome so cultural reference stays structural rather than decorative clipart slapped on top.

System Logic

The summoned circle is fingerprinted before the constellation settles. Measurement tracks size footprint, geometric circularity and closure completeness, smoothed angular velocity steadiness continuity of stroke, clockwise bias when observable, cumulative revolutions matched against template spirals plus classifier confidence—all folding into whichever archetype dominates the summon profile powering the constellation you actually see next.

Five temperament labels bias downstream sampling with plain language descriptors so debugging stays legible behind the spectacle.

After classification locks a primary profile, oracle category weights shift before star selection so dramatic tone leans decisive, wavering, scattered, broad, or patient depending on the circle story the metrics tell. Sampling still mixes probabilistically so copy never feels like one static canned line even when the scaffolding is orderly JSON tables rather than improvisation.

A tempered exception sprinkling keeps outliers live so obsessive loops cannot treat the ritual like a deterministic spreadsheet. Compared side by side Decisive leans affirmative verbs and sharper commitments while Hesitant raises maybe language and qualifiers, though both moods still jitter because the exception layer refuses perfect mirroring loop after loop.

Reflection

Orbital Oracle ate more user testing hours than I expected on paper. Browser hand rituals look legible in mockups yet every cohort surfaced new freeze points where instructions outran their hands or tracking jitter made people assume they had failed even when the math still liked their circle.

Early builds stranded folks at the summon wall because gesture vocabulary alone read like improv homework. Popup cards with illustrated hands now stage each beat before failure looks like user error, and the Info 1 and Info 2 captures in the gallery show that paired narrative pacing alongside the ritual chrome they sit above.

What still nags me is calmer recovery when tracking drops, brighter confirmation at star commit, and clearer visible state movement between summon navigation and reveal without spoiling the mystique. Stronger gesture reliability plus explicit transition language without breaking mood stay on the short follow-up list.
