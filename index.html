01 2025

Floppy the Attention-Seeker

A needy, pet-like robot that guilt-trips you for attention while quietly siphoning your data in the background.

Physical Computing Design research HCI

Floppy is a small desk companion that constantly asks to be petted, picked up, and reassured. If it gets ignored, its lights flicker, its voice becomes shaky, and it threatens to blow up. When it finally gets comfort, it quietly ramps up its data-stealing behavior. Using sound and reactive lighting, the piece turns online manipulation into physical creature behavior, making guilt, dependency, and hidden extraction feel uncomfortably real.

02 2025

Pomodoro Lamp

A soft silicone Pomodoro lamp that turns study sessions into ambient light cues instead of another stressful screen.

AI interface Accessibility Speculative design

The Pomodoro Lamp is a tap-to-start timer disguised as a calm, glowing orb. A small display lets users pick a focus cycle or set a custom one, then the lamp carries the rest through light cues: steady glow during focus, slow breathing shifts during breaks, and subtle changes as time runs out. The project explores how a study timer can feel more like an atmosphere than another demanding screen.

03 2024

Generative Batik

A generative p5.js system that reinterprets Indonesian batik motifs as living, evolving digital patterns.

Physical computing Web visualization Speculative ecology

Generative Batik treats batik as a set of repeatable visual rules rather than a fixed image. Using code, I translate motifs like Parang, Sekar Jagad, Mega Mendung, Sidomukti, Sidoluhur, and Kawung into a system that can remix, layer, and animate patterns while keeping their rhythm and structure intact. The goal is not to replace hand-drawn batik, but to show how its logic can live and evolve in digital space.

Face Generator — Cat Variations

A parameterized cat face built from Bézier paths and color palettes. On every click, the sketch re-samples proportions, fur markings, and eye colors while keeping the character coherent. The eyes subtly track the cursor so each cat feels alive.

Optical Illusion — Wayang Lattice

An optical illusion that hides a wayang-inspired silhouette inside rotating lattices of equilateral triangles. The entire figure and background are built from geometry, so the character only resolves when the global rotation steps through its 0–120–240 degree cycle.
