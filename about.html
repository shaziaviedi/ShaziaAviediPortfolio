About

Design and Technology student exploring creative coding, interaction, and stories that sit between Jakarta and New York.

Education

BFA Design and Technology, Parsons School of Design. Courses include Critical Computation Lab, Core Lab Objects, Device Art, and other studios that link code to physical and narrative experiments.

Interests

I’m interested in generative systems, time-based media, and how small interfaces can hold cultural memory. A lot of my work pulls from Indonesian visual language, like batik and wayang, and places it inside browser-based experiments or speculative devices.

Relationship with technology

Coding feels like another drawing tool. I care less about perfectly polished software and more about how interfaces feel when someone touches them, scrolls them, or pauses with them for a moment. This portfolio collects my experiments from Critical Computation Lab and shows how my understanding of JavaScript and p5.js evolved this semester.
